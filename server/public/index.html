How it works

Three steps to save a life

No Wi-Fi, no cell towers, no internet needed. Pure Bluetooth mesh — works anywhere.

01
📱
Install & Set Up
Download MeshAlert on Android. Enter your name and medical info in the profile — it gets broadcast with every SOS so first responders know who you are.
02
📡
Mesh Connects Automatically
The app advertises via BLE and scans for nearby peers. Devices within 50m form a mesh automatically — no setup needed. Each device acts as a relay node.
03
🆘
SOS Propagates the Mesh
Press the SOS button. Your alert — GPS coordinates, blood group, voice note — relays hop-by-hop through every nearby device until it reaches a gateway with internet.
Features

Built for the worst case scenario

Every feature is designed to work when infrastructure fails — because that's exactly when you need it most.

📡
BLE Mesh Network
Pure Bluetooth Low Energy GATT protocol. No Google Play Services, no internet required. Works on all Android devices with BLE support.
Always Offline
🔴
One-Tap SOS Broadcast
Tap to broadcast. Shake three times for instant SOS. Long-press to skip countdown. Your alert propagates through up to 10 relay hops.
Emergency Response
📍
GPS Location Sharing
Every SOS includes precise GPS coordinates. Pinned live on the dashboard map so rescue teams know exactly where to go — without needing to call.
Location Aware
🎙
Voice SOS Notes
Hold to record a voice message, attach it to your SOS. Recipients can play it back directly in the app or dashboard — no typing needed in a crisis.
Hands-Free
🩸
Medical Profile Broadcast
Blood group, allergies, conditions, emergency contacts — all embedded in your SOS payload. First responders get critical info the moment your signal arrives.
Triage Ready
🔷
QR Identity Card
Your profile generates a scannable QR code. A first responder can scan your phone screen — even if you're unconscious — to instantly access your medical info.
Scan & Identify
💓
Heartbeat Ping
Every 5 minutes, each device broadcasts a heartbeat. Responders can see which survivors are still active and where they are, even without a formal SOS.
Passive Monitoring
🗺
Live Command Dashboard
Real-time map of all SOS alerts, live feed, CSV export, and SSE-powered updates. Deploy it on Railway and your team sees everything the moment it happens.
Command Center
🔒
Foreground Service
An Android foreground service keeps BLE scanning alive even when the screen is off. Battery optimization exemption prevents Doze mode from throttling the mesh.
Always On

Each message has a UUID. Devices track seen IDs to prevent loops. TTL decrements each hop.
